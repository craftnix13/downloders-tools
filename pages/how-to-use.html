How to Use SaveIt.Pro

Everything you need to know about downloading videos from any platform in 3 easy steps.

Steps

3-Step Download Process

1

Find Your Video

Open Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or any supported platform. Find the video, reel, short, or story you want to save. Make sure the content is public.

2

Copy the Link

On mobile: tap the three-dot menu (⋮) or share icon and select "Copy Link". On desktop: copy the URL directly from your browser address bar.

3

Paste & Download

Select the right tool for your platform on our homepage. Paste the copied link into the input box and click the Download button. Your video will be ready in seconds!
